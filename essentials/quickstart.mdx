## Step by step guide

<Steps>
  <Step 
    title="Authorize the bot"
  >
    Click on the card below to authorize Google Analytics bot on your Discord server.

    <Card
      title="Authorize Google Analytics bot"
      icon="discord"
      href="https://discord.com/api/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1197923779213533285&permissions=8&scope=bot"
    >
      Click here to authorize the bot on your Discord server.
    </Card>

    Admin permission is required for now. As you complete the authorization flow, you should see the bot appear on your Discord server among the Members list at the right of the screen. You can search for it or click on the user-group icon.

    <Info>
      Google Analytics bot appearing on your server
      <img
        style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
        src="/images/bot-appearing.png"
      />
    </Info>

  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Go to settings">

    Type `/settings`in the message writing input from any channel. You'll see 2 settings command. One to set Google Analytics, the other to set your onboarding channel. 

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/images/settings-command.png"
    />

    Select `/settings google analytics`

    <Warning>
      If you can't find the Google Analytics bot commands, refresh your all page.
    </Warning>
    
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your Google Analytics">

    The command will prompt 2 parameters:`tag-id` and `api-secret`

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/images/ga-settings.png"
    />

    <Steps>
      <Step 
        title="get your measurement-id"
        icon="google"
      >

        <Warning>
          If you don't have Google Analytics already, create a new one, it's free.
        </Warning>

        Go to your [Google Analytics website](https://analytics.google.com) to copy your measurement-id.

        <img
          style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
          src="/images/data-stream-config-highlight.png"
        />

        Paste it in the `/settings google analytics` command

        <img
          style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
          src="/images/ga-settings-measurement-id.png"
        />

        **Can't find your measurment-id ?**  
        Follow this step by step video to get this page

        - Go to the Administration dashboard
        - Look for the `Data stream` page. 
        - Click on your `Data stream` to open the configuration page

        <video
          style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
          src="/images/ga-measurement-id.mp4"
        />

      </Step>
      <Step 
        title="get your api-secret"
        icon="key"
      >

        On the same [Google Analytics website](https://analytics.google.com) page where you found your measurement-id, look below for the `API secrets for measurement protocole` page.

        <img
          style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
          src="/images/data-stream-config-highlight.png"
        />

        Create and copy a new `api-secret` for the Google Analytics bot.

        <img
          style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
          src="/images/ga-new-api-secret.png"
        />

        Paste it in the `/settings google analytics` command

        <img
          style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
          src="/images/ga-settings-api-secret.png"
        />


        **Can't find your api-secret ?**  
        Follow this step by step video to create a new `api-secret`

        - Click to open the `API secrets for measurement protocole` page.
        - Click the top right button to `read and accept the terms` (option).
        - Click on `Create`.
        - Type a name for your `api-secret` (exemple: 'Discord-bot')
        - Confirm to close the drawer
        - Copy your new `api-secret`

        <video
          style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
          src="/images/ga-api-secret-creation.mp4"
        />

      </Step>

      <Step 
        title="send the command"
        icon="check"
      >

        Hit `Enter`to send the command and wait for the confirmatio message ✅

        <img
          style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
          src="/images/ga-settings-confirmation.png"
        />

      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your onboarding channel">

  Finally, type `/settings` in the message writing input from any channel again.

  <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/images/settings-command-channel.png"
    />

  Select the `/settings onboarding channel`command this time.

  <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/images/settings-channel-selection.png"
    />

  Select your server's onboarding channel.

  <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="/images/settings-channel-message.png"
    />

  Hit `Enter`to send the command and wait for the confirmatio message ✅

  </Step>
</Steps>
