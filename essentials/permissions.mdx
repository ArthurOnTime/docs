## Invite tracker

Accessing invites from a Discord server require the `Manage Server` permission.

## Channels tracker

Tracking all channels from a Discord server require the `Admin` permission.
This allows the bot to automatically add itself to every channels.

<Note>You can remove `Admin` permission and manually invite the bot to every private channels you wish to track</Note>
